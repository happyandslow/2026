SoCC'26 is 17th edition of the annual ACM Symposium on Cloud Computing, the premier conference on cloud computing. It brings together researchers, developers, users, and practitioners interested in cloud computing, and is the only conference co-sponsored by the ACM Special Interest Groups on Management of Data (SIGMOD) and on Operating Systems (SIGOPS). As in prior SoCC conferences, SoCC'26 will foster connections between the academic and industrial communities operating in the cloud space.

SoCC'26 will take place in Singapore from November 18th-20th..
